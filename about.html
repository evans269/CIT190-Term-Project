Crazy Mocha: Do you like cats and caffeine? If so, then this interactive game is for you. Select a caffeinated beverage to get the finish line as quickly as possible. Each beverage moves "Crazy Mocha" at various speeds based upon the amount of caffeine in each serving.

Choose a Path: We make choices each day. What do your choices say about you? Play this game to see what your choices reveal about you. This game is for entertainment only and should not be taken seriously.

Rock, Paper, Scissors: This classic game actually goes back to the Han Dynasty in China around as early as 206 BC! Some play to pass time while others use it as a "win/lose" game. (The Surprising Psychology of Rock-Paper-Scissors)
